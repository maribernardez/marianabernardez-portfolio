HI THERE!

I am a Venezuelan graphic designer based in New York City with a background in journalism, policy-making and community-building. My creative process is driven by an understanding of needs, strong conceptual thinking and an openness to multidisciplinary areas of knowledge. I believe in having a holistic approach, and in the power of visual storytelling to connect people and spark conversations.
