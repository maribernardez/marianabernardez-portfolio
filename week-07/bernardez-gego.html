Gego - Art Catalogue

Gertrud Goldschmidt was an artist and a conceptualizer. GEGO is structured around her ideas, and designed by inspiration of her language of space and line. This perfect-bound book is 110 pages long, focused on imagery, and includes Guy Brett's essay "Gego's Forcefields" and risographed papers.
