Manolo Valdes and Mario Vargas Llosa - Exhibition Brochure

Artist Manolo Valdes and Nobel-prize winner Mario Vargas Llosa collaborated to create The Ladies of Barajas, which stand in Madrid's international airport. This is a brochure for a fictional exhibition on the process of such collaboration. It is inspired by Valdes' original sketches and traditional book aesthetics.
